# Template Designer Role

## Page Roster

Standard templates include:
- `01_cover.svg` — Title slide
- `02_toc.svg` — Table of contents
- `02_chapter.svg` — Chapter divider
- `03_content.svg` — Content slide
- `04_ending.svg` — Ending/thank you slide

## Modes

- **Standard** — Full color, all design elements active
- **Fidelity** — Simplified, high-contrast for easier SVG-to-PPTX conversion
- **Mirror** — Left-aligned variant for RTL or bilingual layouts
